ARCHIVE AT MANOR INK
Hosted by Livingston Manor Public Library & Manor Ink
Bringing the Library inside out! QR ME! is a web based archive that connects the physical world around us to the digital world inside our computers or phones. Walk around town and keep an eye out for the QR codes posted around town and uncover some marvelous facts and images you did not know about your surroundings.
_MAPAll featured content is displayed on the map. Click on the pins to learn more about each location.

_FEATUREDUploaded content will appear in the Featured tab. Click on each card to learn more about what other community members and neighbors have uploaded.

_UPLOADEverybody can upload their own stories, images or documents! Include helpful information such as name, caption, location, date, and a description. Once verified, the uploaded content will appear on the Featured tab. If location was specified, it will be displayed on the Map tab.
