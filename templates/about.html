Community Building through Knowledge Sharing

This PIT aims to help connect you with your local community.

  • Map

    • All featured content is displayed on the map. Click on the pins to learn more about each location.

  • Featured

    • Uploaded content will appear in the Featured tab. Click on each card to learn more about what other community members uploaded.

  • Upload

    • You may also feel free to upload your own stories. Include helpful information such as name, caption, location, date, and a description. Once verified, your uploaded content will appear on the Featured tab. If you specified a location, it will be displayed on the Map tab.

Enjoy!
